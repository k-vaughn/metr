METR - Workshop 12

METR Deployment Part II

Questions for METR Workshop 12: METR Deployment Part II

The twelfth METR workshop will include focus on the following questions:

  • Might METR be a prerequisite for supporting specific cooperative ITS operations?
  • What challenges exist with phasing in the use of METR?
  • What is the process for jurisdictions to phase in support of METR?
  • Should there be a way for a jurisdiction to initiate a "discovery" mode to crowd-source or otherwise import readings reported from sensor-equipped user systems in the field?
  • What protections does METR need to provide to prevent/recover from false regulatory information?
  • To what extent is there a need for remote systems (e.g., vehicles departing for a long drive, lawyers, insurance companies, fleet operators) to access METR information?
  • What happens to deployments when we need to update the METR standard(s)?
  • What types of vehicles (if any) might be required to support METR once a region achieves a certain maturity level?
