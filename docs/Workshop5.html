Questions for METR Workshop 5: Vehicle Operations

The fifth METR workshop will include focus on the following questions:

  • Do vehicles need METR to filter operations? (e.g., by vehicle classification)
  • What regulations do users need to know for other users? (e.g., what other types of users also are allowed to use this facility?)
  • What information does a vehicle need to determine the regulations to which it must conform?
  • Does a vehicle need to know the legal definition of road types and usage allowances?
  • How detailed should METR information be? (e.g., does METR need to identify the position of each stop sign or just convey what a stop sign looks like)?
  • What categories of information should be standardized so that jurisdictions can identify their level of support?
  • What information does a vehicle need to determine if it is allowed to operate on a facility?
  • To what extent do vehicles need to be able to learn regional conventions for traffic control devices? (e.g., colours of specialized lanes)
  • What information do vehicles need from METR to coordinate the recognition of a new traffic control device being installed?
