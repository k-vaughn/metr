Questions for METR Workshop 1: METR Operations

The first METR workshop will include an overview of METR and focus on the following questions:

  • What are reasonable constraints to impose on METR related to the use of wireless data?
  • What trustworthiness measures does a user need to satisfy?
  • What operational modes does METR need to support?
  • How much time is needed to advertise new notices?
  • How should information be delivered (e.g., push vs pull)?
  • How do users know that they have received all current information?
  • What is needed to support multi-version interoperability?
