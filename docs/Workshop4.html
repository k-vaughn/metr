Questions for METR Workshop 4: Electronic Regulation Conflicts

The fourth METR workshop will include focus on the following questions:

  • What are the legal and enforcement implications if there is a discrepancy between the posted and electronic regulations?
  • Should a user system report a discrepancy between posted and electronic regulations?
  • What should the METR system do when it learns of a conflict?
  • What should the disseminator, collector, and translator do when they learn of a conflict?
  • How should conflicts between permanent and temporary regulations be resolved?
  • What is the expected validity period for downloaded regulations?
  • What happens when the validity period is exceeded?
  • What other issues do we need to consider?
