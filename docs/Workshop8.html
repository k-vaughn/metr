Questions for METR Workshop 8: Campus Regulations

The eighth METR workshop will include focus on the following questions:

  • What is the process for a campus facility to implement its own regulations within METR?
  • What is the boundary between METR and mapping systems?
  • Do campus regulations need to be validated in any way (legal authority and legality of law)?
  • Should METR distinguish between regulations that do not meet normal guidelines? (e.g., speed limit of 17)
  • Are there any additional needs to allow METR to better support the adherence to and enforcement of campus regulations?
  • Does METR need to support any special considerations for (e.g., on-street) parking regulations, such as those for residents only, decal required, duration limits, or visitor tag?
  • What implications are there for regulators issuing parking permits (e.g., is there a need for an electronic parking decal or visitor tag)?
  • What special considerations might METR need to support in relation to on-street parking and temporary changes that might be required (e.g., for snow ploughing, construction, etc.)?
  • What other issues do we need to consider?
